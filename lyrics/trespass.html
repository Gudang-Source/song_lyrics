Rich Brian talks about how his newfound fame has got people from his past trying to reconnect with him and feed off his fame. The intro about his grandmother is mostly satirical since he has expressed respect for his family, namely his parents, in previous works.

In response, Brian chooses to ignore those from his past and tell them to stop asking him to catch up. He talks about how crazy he gets and compares himself to an animal.
